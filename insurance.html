MFG Home Communities

Commercial

Commercial Liability: Injury and Property Damage, Premises and Operations, Broad Form Property Damage, Blanket Contractual, Personal Injury.

Premium: Based on the number of sites or gross receipts.

Inland Marine: and Equipment Floater Comprehensive Auto Liability.

Physical Damage: Bodily Injury, Property Damage, and Liability Coverage, Owned, Leased, Hired Auto Liability, Medical Payments, Uninsured/Underinsured Motorists Coverage. Physical Damage: Comprehensive and Collision Coverage.

Manufactured Homes

Personal

Coverage that protects you and your home.

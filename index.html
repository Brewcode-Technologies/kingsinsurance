About Company

Welcome to our website

King Insurance is a family-owned and operated agency that has been servicing the Manufactured Housing and Recreational Vehicle Industries for more than 40 years. We are the only agency in the Midwest that focuses on coverages for manufactured homes/communities and recreational vehicles, both personal and commercial. Additionally, we serve individuals seeking home, auto, and life insurance. We work with several carriers in order to offer the most competitive rates, and are committed to a high standard of excellence in all that we do, establishing a firm relationship of mutual trust with each client.
